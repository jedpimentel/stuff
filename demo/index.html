Random Demos

Some demos might not work well (or at all) on your current device. This ain't a complete list.

Grip to move + Physics

Custom movement controls, plus the aframe-physics-system library.

Positional Audio

A-Frame supports positional audio out of the box

Pointy Line Ball Thing

A bunch of spheres, laid out in a sphere, with lines pointing out from them.

NumBall

White circle with "000" in it in black text. It's closer to the camera than the background. On 2D-displays the ball looks bigger than it is because of perspective, on 3D-displays you can tell it's just being rendered closer o your face.

touch-controls

Move around a 3D scene using an touchscreen joystick. Part of my effort to make VR scenes accessible on 2D-touchscreens as a 3D environment.

webcam

Webcam passthrough stuff. Originally meant to try out what passthrough would look like on a cardboard-class headset. Doesn't work on Oculus Quest, prob since the cameras are for tracking, not video.
